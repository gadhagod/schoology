### updates
List your schools.

**Syntax** \
`schoology schools [OPTIONS]`

**Options** 
- `--limit INTEGER`
 - Limit of results
 - Not required, defaults to ten
- `--id`
 - Show ID
 - Not required
- `--name`
 - Show name
 - Not required
- `--location`
 - Show location
 - Not required
- `--postal-code`
 - Show postal code
 - Not required
- `--website`
 - Show website
 - Not required
- `phone`
 - Show phone number
 - Not required

`--id`, `--name`, `--location`, `--postal-code`, `--website`, and `--phone` are defaults unless at least one is specicied.

**Description** \
Echoes schools and metadata.
